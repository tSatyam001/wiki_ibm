A.P.J Abdul Kalam

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and politician who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007.

Early Life

He was born on 15 October 1931 in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu. He studied physics and aerospace engineering.

Career Highlights

  • Worked with DRDO and ISRO
  • Known as the Missile Man of India
  • Received Bharat Ratna in 1997
